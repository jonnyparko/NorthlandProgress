WHAT IS NORTHLAND PROGRESS?

Northland Progress is a grassroots coalition supporting the adoption of forward-thinking policies to benefit the people of the northern Kansas City metro area. Northland Progress was created to inform Northland voters on local and statewide issues that will affect their lives, to help progressive Northlanders connect for a common purpose, and to advocate for policies that will lead to the continued advancement and long-term prosperity of the region. Initial focus will be on issues affecting the citizens of Platte, Clay, Buchanan, Clinton, and Ray counties.

KEY ISSUES

Northland Progress relies on its members for direction.

While the board of directors will make final decisions, the members of Northland Progress will have considerable influence over issue advocacy and priority determinations.

VOTING RIGHTS

NP supports fair electoral processes and opposes voter suppression laws and partisan political gerrymandering in Missouri. We will work to educate citizens on voting laws, register voters and update voter registrations, and mobilize Northland citizens to ensure they can participate in their government.

HEALTHCARE RIGHTS

In this great country of ours, healthcare should not be a privilege for the fortunate few, but a right for all. NP supports measures that will expand access to affordable healthcare for all Northland citizens and give women (and their doctors) control over their reproductive health.

ENVIRONMENTAL RESPONSIBILITY

The natural beauty of the Northland is one of our greatest resources. NP will support clean air and water initiatives, improvement of local recycling programs, and policies encouraging the use of alternative and sustainable energy sources.

WORKERS RIGHTS

The middle class is the backbone of a strong economy, but many hardworking Missourians are facing threats to their standard of living. NP supports fair wages and safe workplaces, and the right of workers to organize to demand a fair shake.

PUBLIC EDUCATION

We believe everyone deserves access to the same quality education. NP will work to secure high quality and well-funded public education for all Northland children.

INFRASTRUCTURE IMPROVEMENTS

Strong regions need strong infrastructure (such as highways and bridges). NP will work to ensure that Northland residents and businesses can rely on safe transportation for years to come.

COMMON SENSE GUN LAWS

It is our mission to reduce the amount of deaths in this country by firearm. We support common sense gun legislation that both protects our children and our constitutional rights.
